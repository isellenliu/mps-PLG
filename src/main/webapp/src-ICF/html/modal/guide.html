Guide
  • Rules
    • Minimum bet is
      100
      .
    • Betting window is 3 to 5 mins. There are 3 game status; OPEN, LAST CALL and CLOSED. Once betting is CLOSED, bets will not be accepted.
    • Payout style: Sports betting.
    • Payout computation (odds): % based on total bet per side Example: 100 bet at 180% wins 180.
    • If fight is a draw, bets are returned.
    • Fight will be voided if odds is lower than
      130%
      , and the bets will be reimbursed.
