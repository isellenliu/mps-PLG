SABONG/COCKFIGHTING CHALLENGE

SABONG a cockfighting event when Meron accept any challenge from any challenger that forecome to challenge their prize cock to for a fight with high amount of wager on the line. The Wala challenge them with a smaller center bet laid.

  1. Meron

    The MERON issues the challenge and and has bigger center bet laid , making them the slight favourite. Starting odds at 0.80

  2. Wala

    The WALA challenge the meron and has smaller center bet laid ,making the the least favourite. Starting odds at -0.94

  3. BDD

    "Both Dead Draw" Both Cocks die and declare draw , Odds at 1:8. Meron and Wala bets will be refunded.

  4. FTD

    "Full Time Draw" Both Cocks Alive and declare draw, after Ten minute limit COUNTDOWN .Meron ,Wala and BDD bets will be refunded. Cambodia match result will follow arena decision if fight time more than 10 minute
